1. Service Description

Tax Integrity Monitor is a Shopify app that scans your store's order history to detect tax discrepancies caused by partial refunds, undercollection relative to known state base tax rates, and accidental tax collection in jurisdictions where you are not registered.

The App operates in read-only mode. It does not modify, edit, or delete any orders, refunds, tax settings, or financial data. All remediation actions (adjusting tax settings, issuing corrections) are performed by you, the merchant, outside of this App.

These factors may materially affect whether a detected discrepancy represents an actual compliance issue.
  • False positives — Some findings may identify discrepancies that are intentional, expected, or already accounted for in your tax filings.
  • False negatives — The App may not detect all tax discrepancies, particularly from manual refunds without line items, orders with estimated taxes, or jurisdictions with unparseable tax line titles.
  • Unauditable orders — Orders with manual refunds that lack line-item details cannot be analyzed by the App and are flagged as "unauditable." This designation indicates a technical limitation of available data, not a finding of tax discrepancy or non-compliance. No action should be taken based solely on an "unauditable" designation without reviewing the underlying order in Shopify.
  • Orders with estimated taxes — Orders where Shopify has not yet finalized tax calculations (flagged as "estimated taxes" in Shopify's API) are excluded from scanning. These orders will not appear in findings.
  • State base rates — Undercollection detection compares collected rates against known state-level base sales tax rates. Local rates (county, city, district) are not included. Rate data may not reflect recent rate changes, emergency legislation, or pending adjustments. Always verify current rates against official state revenue department sources before acting on undercollection findings.
  • Nexus configuration accuracy — Unregistered collection detection depends entirely on the nexus states you declare through the App's settings. The App cannot detect collection in unregistered states that you have not declared as nexus states. You are responsible for accurately maintaining your nexus configuration. We are not responsible for missed findings caused by incomplete or inaccurate nexus declarations.
  • The App does not modify your orders, tax settings, or financial data. You are solely responsible for any actions you take based on scan findings.
